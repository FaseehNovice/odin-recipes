Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a flat, baked bread dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and other ingredients like meat, vegetables, and herbs. It originated in Naples in the 19th century and is known for its quick, high-temperature baking, often in a wood-fired oven, resulting in a hot, delicious pie.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups (355 ml) warm water (105°F-115°F)
  • 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 3 3/4 cups (490g) bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil (omit if cooking pizza in a wood-fired pizza oven)
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Cornmeal
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mushrooms, very thinly sliced if raw, otherwise first sautéed
  • Bell peppers, stems and seeds removed, very thinly sliced
  • Italian pepperoncini, thinly sliced
  • Sliced black olives
  • Chopped fresh basil
  • Onions, thinly sliced raw or caramelized

Steps

  1. Proof the yeast
  2. Make and knead the pizza dough
  3. Let the dough rise
  4. Preheat the pizza stone
  5. Divide the dough into 2 balls
  6. Prep the toppings
  7. Flatten the dough ball, and stretch out into a round
  8. Brush the dough top with olive oil
  9. Sprinkle the pizza peel with cornmeal, put flattened dough on topSprinkle the pizza peel with cornmeal, put flattened dough on top
  10. Spread with tomato sauce and sprinkle with toppings
  11. Slide pizza into the oven
  12. Bake
